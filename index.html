Hi there! This website was made by Dean, Maxene, and Ervin, led by our coach Mr. Ruben B. Capisnon Jr. We are the representatives of Far Eastern College- Silang for Quake Quest 2024. This is a product of our research "Interactive Mapping of Significant Earthquakes for Assessing Localized Seismic Hazards" that aims to visualize significant earthquakes and its harm done to the community. By this, we hope to raise awareness for risk reduction and preparedness, and connect with a broader Filipino audience.
